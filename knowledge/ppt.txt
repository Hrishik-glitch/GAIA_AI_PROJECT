Making AI Sustainable First
EcoMind Data Cells & LumoCore Reactors
Instead of energy-hungry cooling systems, AI data centers become living
ecosystems:
•

Transform traditional, energy-hungry data centers into living, eco-efficient
ecosystems.

•

Algae-filled tanks absorb server heat and CO₂, producing clean biofuel in
return.

•

Underground airflow tunnels circulate cool air naturally, minimizing powerhungry cooling systems.

•

AI workload scheduling automatically shifts computing to hours with peak
solar or wind energy availability.

•

LumoCore Reactors monitor energy demand and maintain balanced thermal
and data flow.

•

greenhouses, forming a closed-loop energy system that benefits both AI and
the planet.

Result: Data centres transform from energy consumers to net producers.
